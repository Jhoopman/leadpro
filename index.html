FAQ

Common questions.

Everything contractors ask before they sign up. Still have questions? Email us at hstratigies@gmail.com

Will the AI sound robotic on the phone?

No. LeadPro uses modern conversational AI with natural speech patterns, pauses, and inflection. Most callers assume they're speaking with a human receptionist. The agent introduces itself as your assistant and handles the conversation professionally from start to finish.

How long does setup take?

Most contractors are live in under 10 minutes. You create an account, paste in your website URL (we scrape it to train your agent), set your hours and service area, and forward your number. That's it. No technical setup required.

Do I need to change my business phone number?

No. You keep your existing number. LeadPro assigns you a local number, and you set up a conditional call forward from your current number — so when you can't answer, LeadPro picks up. You can also use the LeadPro number directly if you prefer.

What if a caller has a question the AI can't answer?

The agent is trained on your specific business — your services, pricing, and FAQs. For anything truly out of scope, it gracefully takes a message and lets the caller know you'll follow up personally. You'll get an instant notification with the full transcript so you can call back right away.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, absolutely. No contracts, no cancellation fees. You can cancel from your account dashboard at any time and your plan stays active until the end of the billing period. We also offer a full 14-day free trial, no credit card required.
