FAQ

Common questions.

Everything contractors ask before they sign up. Still have questions? Email us at hello@useleadpro.net

Will my customers know they're texting a bot?

They'll know it's automated — and they won't care. What customers hate is calling and never hearing back. LeadPro responds in under 60 seconds, every time. That's faster than any human receptionist.

What if I already have an answering service?

Answering services take messages. LeadPro books jobs. There's a hard difference between "I'll have him call you back" and "Your appointment is Thursday at 10am." What does your answering service cost? LeadPro is $97/mo and it actually converts.

I need to qualify leads myself. How does AI know my business?

LeadPro doesn't close the job — you do. It captures their name, address, what they need, and their availability. You still make the final call. What it stops is the lead going cold while you're finishing a job.

How long does setup take?

Ten minutes. Forward your business number to your LeadPro number, add your business name, and you're live. No IT. No training. No 90-day onboarding.

Does it work with my current tools?

Yes. LeadPro works alongside Jobber, HouseCall Pro, ServiceTitan — whatever you use. It handles the calls they miss. Think of it as the front door; your existing tools run the rest.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime, no questions asked. We're confident you won't want to once you see your first recovered job hit the dashboard.
